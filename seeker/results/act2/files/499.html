You can also use AND and OR for more complex searches. "Netscape AND Apple" will give you all stories that mention both companies. "Netscape OR Apple" will give you all stories that mention one company, the other, or both. You can combine AND and OR for more complex searches. "Netscape AND Apple OR Netscape AND IBM" will give you stories that mention Netscape and either IBM or Apple. You can also use NOT to eliminate stories from your search.
